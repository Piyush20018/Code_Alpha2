Welcome To Challenge-response Authentication Part (CAPTCHA):

here you will be provided with different types of captcha inorder to detect that you are human, so please do introduce yourself with those different types of captchas

comparing different types of CAPTCHA services along with their pros and cons:

CAPTCHA Type pros cons
Image-based CAPTCHA - Familiar to users - Vulnerable to OCR attacks
- Offers various complexity levels - Inaccessible to visually impaired users
- Resistant to basic bot attacks and Can be customized for branding - User frustration with difficult puzzles
Text-based CAPTCHA - Inclusive for visually impaired users - Prone to text recognition attacks
- Less frustrating for most users - Limited difficulty range
- Offers multilingual options and Simpler for human verification - May still be cracked by advanced bots
Audio CAPTCHA - Accessible to visually impaired users - Requires audio support and playback
- Resistant to text recognition attacks - User inconvenience due to audio playback
- Challenges bots with sound recognition - Background noise can hinder audio decoding
Checkbox CAPTCHA - Extremely user-friendly - Less effective against sophisticated bots
- Requires a single interaction - Prone to false positives
- Fast and easy to solve and Low risk of user frustration - Can be bypassed by clever automation
Token CAPTCHA -Minimal user effort - Limited security against advanced attacks
- Can be seamlessly integrated - May not effectively challenge determined bots
- Less intrusive to user experience - Overuse can lead to predictability
